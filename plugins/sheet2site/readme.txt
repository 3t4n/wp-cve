Sheet2Site - Embed your Google Sheet into your WordPress website.

We now bring the power of Sheet2Site to your existing website with Sheet2Site WordPress Plugin.
Make any webpage dynamic, by adding your Sheet2Site page and manage it from Google Spreadsheets.

Whether it's a time table, product inventory, job listings, or anything else you can think of. Manage it with ease through Google Spreadsheet powered by Sheet2Site.
