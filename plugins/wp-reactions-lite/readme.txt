== Installation ==
1. Navigate to Plugins > Add New.
2. Click the Upload Plugin button at the top of the screen.
3. Select the zip file from your local filesystem.
4. Click the Install Now button.

When installation is complete, you’ll see “Plugin installed successfully.” Click the Activate Plugin button at the bottom of the page.

5. Once WP Reactions plugin is activated, navigate to plugin dashboard to enter license key. 


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I choose the page and posts I want Reactions on? =
Yes. You can easily do this by choosing manual mode while customizing your reactions. After this option is selected, you can go to each page and manage your reactions.

= Do I need any coding skills for this plugin? =
No Coding Skills Required. We built WP Reactions for all users at all skill levels. Our step wizard will guide you through to make sure your reactions look great on your pages and posts.

= Are these emojis Gifs or Lottie Animation? =
Our emojis are the highest quality Lottie animation. They are fast loading and deliver a great user experience for your audience. Our emojis look fantastic on any color background.

= What Social Sharing platforms are integrated? =
You can choose from Facebook, Twitter and Email sharing. To add social options, please visit our main website.

= Can I disable Social Sharing? =
Yes, we realize that you may have other plugins for social sharing activated, therefore, we give you the option to disable this feature when customizing your setup.

= Can I make up my own user counts? =
Yes, you can manage this option at the page and post level while editing. You will find our admin panel at the bottom and you can easily update and manage your user counts.

= Do I need to connect my site to your platform with an API? =
No API required. Simply install WP Reactions  and your emojis are ready to go!

= Does this plugin provide analytics on my users reactions? =
Yes. You can access your analytical data on each post or page that your reactions are activated on. Simply scroll down while editing to see the WP Reactions Dashboard.

= Do you offer a Pro Version of this Plugin? =
Yes. We have a tremendous upgrade with more powerful features. You can visit our website for [more info](https://wpreactions.com)

= Can I arrange my emojis in any order I want? =
Yes. With our Drag and Drop feature, you can arrange the order anyway you like.
