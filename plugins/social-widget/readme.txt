A beautiful widget that allow you to add a stylish Facebook like box, twitter follow button and a google +1 button to your sidebar.

== Description ==

Adds a social widget for your social media account to your wordpress-sidebar. I know, there are many plugins to do this, but this one combine the most used social media like facebook, twitter and help your seo with the google +1 button, in a stylish way.


**Features**

*   Fast loading
*   styled facebook likebox
*   easy to use
*   fully customizable


== Installation ==

Copy the directory 'social-widget' to your WordPress '/wp-content/plugins' folder.


1. Activate the 'social widget' plugin in your WordPress admin 'Plugins'
2. Add the 'social widget' to the sidebar from the widget menu


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I find the ID of my fanpage? =

Just go to your facebook-fanpage and take a look at the link "edit page". The link looks like facebook.com/pages/edit/?id=xxxxxxxxxxxxxxx. The number with the 15 digits after ?id= is what you need.
