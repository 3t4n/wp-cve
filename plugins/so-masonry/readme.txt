== Description ==
The SiteOrigin Masonry plugin gives you a widget that you can use to display your posts. It gives you a widget you can add to your [Page Builder](http://wordpress.org/plugins/siteorigin-panels/) pages or sidebar.

Take a look at the demo [here](http://demo.siteorigin.com/origami/masonry-layout/).

[Our newsletter](http://eepurl.com/hbvhM) is a great place to stay up to date with Masonry Widget and the rest of our free WordPress products.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin.
2. [Regenerate your thumbnails](http://wordpress.org/plugins/regenerate-thumbnails/).
3. Add a masonry widget to your sidebar or [Page Builder](http://wordpress.org/plugins/siteorigin-panels/) page.
