**Steps to create slider**

1. Install the slider plugin.
1. Click on Bee Sliders menu
1. Add New slider
1. Add your slider title

**Image Layer**

1. Select your slide layer type image 
1. Select your  slide background image.
1. Select your slide image
1. Select your slide image in animation and out animation
1. define animation delay with multiples of 100's
1. define your image top and left position with  multiples of 100's

**Text Layer**

1. Select your slide layer type text 
1. Type your text.
1. Select your slide text in animation and out animation
1. define animation delay with multiples of 100's
1. define your text top and left position with  multiples of 100's



== Installation ==

1. Upload `bee-slider.zip` from the plugins-->Add New-->Upload
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place [bee-slider id=your slider id] in your page or post
1. Place `<?php echo do_shortcode("[bee-slider id=your slider id]"); ?>` in your templates
