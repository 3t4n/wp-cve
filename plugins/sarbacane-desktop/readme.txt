Avec ce plugin, ajoutez un formulaire opt-in et synchronisez le contenu de votre newsletter dans Sarbacane, notre application d'emailing.

== Description ==

Bienvenue dans ce plugin proposé par Sarbacane Software. Après installation, il vous permettra de synchroniser les données de votre site avec l'application d'email marketing Sarbacane Software.

Les données sont celles relatives à vos comptes (login, prénom, nom, rôle et autres contenus).

Vous serez en mesure de rajouter un widget opt-in que vos visiteurs pourront remplir.

Ce widget, personnalisable (champs, titre...), mettra automatiquement la liste correspondante à jour dans Sarbacane.

Vous aurez accès à vos contenus, médias et thèmes dans la fonctionnalité EmailBuilder de Sarbacane.

= English =

The Sarbacane plugin for email marketing allows you to synchronize your WordPress website data with Sarbacane's email marketing software.

The plugin will automatically synchronize the contact information for your website's users and accounts.
Additionally, you can quickly create a sign-up widget to grow your subscriber list.
These contacts will be automatically added to their own contact lists within Sarbacane.

Want to create email newsletters using your blog content?

This email newsletter plugin from Sarbacane will let you quickly import the text and images from your blogs posts into a responsive email newsletter template.

Configure the plugin to generate "read more" buttons, determine the excerpt length, or even import the entire post.

Let Sarbacane import your WordPress theme's main color scheme to use in your email template, or customize the email on your own to perfectly promote your blog using our drag-and-drop EmailBuilder!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Comment obtenir de l'aide? =

N'hésitez pas à envoyer un mail à l'adresse support@sarbacane.com, nous serons ravis de vous accompagner!

= English =


= Do you offer support? =
Yes, we offer live support Monday through Friday. Please visit our website for contact details or email support@sarbacane.com
= Can I create a signup form for my site? =
Yes, this plugin will let you create a signup widget to be placed in any widget area of your site. Signups will be automatically added to a list in Sarbacane.
= Does the plugin synchronize my ecommerce customers? =
The plugin is designed to synchronize users who have created accounts on your website. If your customers create an account during checkout, they will be synchronized.
= What do you mean the plugin imports post content? =
Connecting Sarbacane to your WordPress website will allow Sarbacane to actually pull the content from your blog posts and create an email newsletter template with it.

You can import the entire post, or just the featured image with a certain amount of characters and a "read more" button.
