The WooCommerce Fygaro Plugin gets online payments with your Local Bank, PayPal, Yappy and Credix up and running within minutes and at the best rates!


== Description ==

Accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, KeyCard, Tasa 0, Mini Cuotas, Yappy, Credix and more from WooCommerce with direct settlement to your local bank account.

*TAKE CREDIT CARD PAYMENTS EASILY AND DIRECTLY SECURE CHECKOUT PAGE WITH YOUR BRAND*

**WHY CHOOSE FYGARO?**
Fygaro can be setup in minutes, there are no hidden costs and only a flat monthly fee. There is no commissions or middleman with funds deposited by your bank into your merchant account daily! (Confirm with your bank).

**MULTIPLE PAYMENT OPTIONS**
With Fygaro, a single plugin can provide you multiple currencies and payment options, including your Local Bank, PayPal, and more.

**Don´t have a Fygaro account yet?**
Sign-up today with Fygaro at www.fygaro.com to get started free! We can even make introductions to local banks in your country.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is the plugin free =

Yes, there is no cost to get the plugin

= Do i need a Fygaro account? =

Yes, you will need a Fygaro plan with Online Payments on the Ideal or Corporate level to use this plugin. Check www.fygaro.com/w/pricing for all options.

= Does it allow multi currency? =

Yes, the plugin will work with the currencies available in your Fygaro account.

= ¿Está disponible en Español? =

Si, el plugin y Fygaro están disponibles tanto en Inglés como en Español.
