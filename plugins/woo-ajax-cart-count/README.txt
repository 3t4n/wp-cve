Woo Ajax Cart Count plugin allow you to show cart total any where in website, by simply place a shortcode.

== Description ==

Woo Ajax Cart Count provides users with the flexibility to showcase the cart total at any desired location on the site. The simple integration involves placing the shortcode **[WooAjaxCartCount]** within the template of your choice. This straightforward process empowers you to seamlessly incorporate and present the current cart total wherever it fits best within your website's design. Enhance your user experience and keep your customers informed about their shopping cart status with this convenient and customizable feature. That's all it takes to effortlessly integrate Woo Ajax Cart Count into your site!

== Installation ==

1. Navigate to the **"Plugins"** menu in WordPress.
2. Search for **"IMS Ajax Cart Count."**
3. Click **"Install."**
4. After installation, click **"Activate."**
5. Proceed to the **"Settings"** menu, then select **"IMS Ajax Cart Count"** for configuration options and shortcode settings.
6. Customize your cart icon (using FontAwesome), choose colors, adjust size, and configure price options to match your preferences.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can we have the option to hide the price on the cart? =

Yes, you can make the change by selecting either "Yes" or "No" from the dropdown menu.

= Where can I find my shortcode? =

Your shortcode is located in the same place where you customize your settings.

= Can this plugin work without WooCommerce? =

No, this plugin only functions with WooCommerce support.
