<li>Shadow in icons.</li>

</ul>

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the Settings menu. Under this, there will be the Fuse Social Menu (Settings>Fuse Social).
4. Configure the settings by putting the URLs to your various social profiles. If you want to show Animation on hover, shadow, open the new tab and size, configure it in Awesome Social Settings.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to make icons animated? =

Go to Settings>Fuse Social from the left side admin menu bar, then click on the Settings tab. Then click on Animation on hover, a text box will appear and then enter the value in seconds,  like enter 0.5, and it will give the delay of 0.5 seconds in Animation.

= Don't want shadow in icons? =

In settings, simply check " Don't use shadow in icons." by default, it's unchecked.
