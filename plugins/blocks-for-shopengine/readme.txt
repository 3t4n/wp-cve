== Description ==

Want a Gutenberg addon for easily building and customizing WooCommerce pages with templates and blocks? Then your wait is over. 

ShopEngine Gutenberg Addon is the most compatible WooCommerce page builder that supports both Gutenberg. Now building your WooCommerce product, shop, cart, checkout, and archive pages is possible with Gutenberg blocks. On top of that, you will get advanced modules like product comparison, quick view, wishlist, etc to create a full-fledged eCommerce store. 

Here in this Gutenberg addon, you will get 5+ templates, 36+ blocks, and 4 modules for building and customizing your eCommerce pages. Besides, with readymade templates, you can create your WooCommerce pages from scratch that will be fully responsive for any device.
