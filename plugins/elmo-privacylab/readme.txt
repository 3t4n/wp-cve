Elmo powered by PrivacyLab is the ultimate GDPR compliant tool for the management of cookie banners and privacy notices for processing carried out by websites.

== Description ==

**Elmo** is PrivacyLab's tool for managing cookies.

It allows you to verify, catalog and properly manage the compliance of your site to the GDPR.

With **Elmo** you keep under control all the consents expressed in the cookie banner, and you can collect them in the "register of consents" required by law.
**Elmo**'s consent log is certified! More help to prove your compliance with GDPR.

With **Elmo** you can automatically create guaranteed and certified treatments and information, and you can also create your own cookie banner starting from the information of the site you already have.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin on your WordPress site
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to the 'Elmo' menu and add your Elmo code
