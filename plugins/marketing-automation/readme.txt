Wordpress integration for Vbout

== Description ==
This integration between Vbout and Wordpress allows you to automate your marketing and tracking on your Wordpress site using your [Vbout account](https://www.vbout.com/login/). Here is a list of the functions available through the plugin:

*   Schedule WP posts and pages to be sent to your social media channels
*   Mask your URL for [tracking clicks](https://www.vbout.com/features/social-media-management/#Link_Tracker) and conversion through social media
*   Schedule posts to be sent as an [email campaign](https://www.vbout.com/features/email-marketing/) to your subscribers on [Vbout.com](https://www.vbout.com/pricing/)
*   Post now or schedule for future posting
*	Embed the Vbout.com tracking code on your site without touching the code
*	Choose your Vbout settings defaults for even quicker publishing

== Installation ==
1. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
