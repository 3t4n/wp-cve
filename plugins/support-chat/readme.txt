== Description ==

NinjaTeam Click to Chat plugin comes with multiple chat apps and messaging platforms to allow your customers to click to contact support. 

With this WP Support All-in-One Floating Widget Plugin, you can create a **click to chat button** on the corner of your website. When clicked or tapped, this floating button expands into various chat apps so that your customers can choose the one they prefer.

[WP Click to Chat Floating Widget - DEMO](https://demo.ninjateam.org/wp-support-all-in-one/)

###üîë HOW IT WORKS

After activating the plugin, you don't need to fill in any license. Just go to **Click to Chat** menu, choose the apps you want to show. For each app/platform, you need to enter the URL or username accordingly.

In the **Display** tab, you can show the floating button on all WordPress pages or your selected pages. You can tweak the design or use your custom icon to make the button stand out or fit with your theme color profiles.

It allows your visitors to start a conversation quickly and assists your customer relationship management (CRM):

- Live chat: Helps customers chat with support agents in real-time, enabling them to get quick responses to their queries.
- Ticketing system: Allows customers to submit support tickets, which are then assigned to support agents who can work on them and provide timely responses.
- Contact forms: Provides a simple way for customers to get in touch through their app in use.
- Social media integration: Collect your leads on various social media platforms.
