== Installation ==

1. **Install:** Install the Bitcoin News either via the WordPress.org plugin directory, or by uploading the files to your server.
2. **Create News Feeds:** Create your Bitcoin News feed by generating a shortcode from the Generate Shortcode tab. Put that shortcode in posts or pages where you want to display your Bitcoin News OR create your Bitcoin News from Appearance > Widgets. From the widgets panel drag the \"BitcoinNews\" widget to the desired sidebar or widget area where you want to show your news feed. Edit the widget features to create/edit your news feed. Choose the name, number of headlines, keywords and other settings.
