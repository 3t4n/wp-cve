== Description ==

Plezi One is a free marketing tool that helps you easily understand your future clients’ digital journey, and transform more traffic into leads with forms.

## Generate leads from your website and use behavioral data to sell

Forget about basic form submission and “contacts”. Plezi tells you where leads come from, what they do on your website, and when they return.

- Create, publish, and automate forms in a few minutes
- Track and understand your leads with analytics
- Take advantage of your existing materials

## One page report to easily understand how you perform online

Get insights week after week on how you perform, with stats to prove it. No need for a dictionary: we keep everything simple.

- Automatic dashboards from traffic to leads
- Top content & SEO rankings
- Applicable data that doesn’t need translation

## About this plugin

This plugin allows you to:

- have the Plezi tracking script through all your website pages
- publish your Plezi forms online, thanks to a tailored Plezi widget (on Gutemberg, Elementor and Divi builders) or simplified shortcodes
- take shortcuts to your Plezi dashboard to follow your website stats

## Who is Plezi?

Plezi is a B2B SaaS that helps marketers generate qualified leads for their sales teams. Founded in 2015 by Renaud de Lacotte and Charles Dolisy, Plezi is the first digital marketing solution which is truly automated.
Plezi is created to simplify your marketing by following each touchpoint of the B2B customer journey. Rather than concentrating on workflows, Plezi can help you generate quality leads through our Smart Campaigns, by segmenting and understanding the interests of your potential customers, reducing the sales cycle and identifying your target market.

## Install

To use the Plezi plugin, you need to have a Plezi One account. [Signup for free here](https://enjoy.plezi.co/signup?utm_medium=referral&utm_source=wordpress&utm_campaign=plezi_one&utm_content=plugin&utm_term=wp-plugins-list).

1. Inside your WordPress account, add the Plezi Plugin and activate it
2. Inside the plugin, connect your Plezi account to WordPress with your API credentials
3. Install Plezi's tracking script on all your website pages
4. Publish your first Plezi form : it can be a contact or demo form, a form to download your latest brochure or white paper -you name it!

## Privacy ##

- This plugin does not collect any user data. Only the tracking code added by this plugin is used by Plezi to collect all sorts of user data. You can learn more about Plezi Privacy [here](https://www.plezi.co/en/legal/?utm_medium=referral&utm_source=wordpress&utm_campaign=plezi_one&utm_content=plugin&utm_term=wp-plugins-list).
- This plugin does not connect to any third-party locations or services, but it does enable Plezi to collect all sorts of data.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are there any pre-requisites to use this plugin? =

To use the Plezi plugin, you need:

- to have a Plezi One account. [Signup here](https://enjoy.plezi.co/signup?utm_medium=referral&utm_source=wordpress&utm_campaign=plezi_one&utm_content=plugin&utm_term=wp-plugins-list).
- connect your Plezi account to your Wordpress account.

= What is this plugin for? =

This plugin allows you to:

- have the Plezi tracking script through all your website pages
- publish your Plezi forms online, thanks to a tailored Plezi widget (on Gutemberg, Elementor and Divi builders) or simplified shortcodes
- take shortcuts to your Plezi dashboard to follow your website stats

= What is Plezi's tracking script for? =

The tracking script allows Plezi to see visits to your website and identify prospects when they return.

= How do I install Plezi tracking script on my website with Wordpress? =

To install Plezi tracking script, follow the instructions on the Setup tab of this plugin.

= How can I get all my Plezi forms in WordPress? =

To find your Plezi forms in WordPress, you’ll need to authenticate your Plezi API keys.

= How can I publish a Plezi form in WordPress? =

If you use Elementor, Gutenberg or Divi, that's fantastic! You’ll find a Plezi Form widget, directly in your page editor. Otherwise, you can copy your form's shortcode here and paste it in your editor.

= How can I use Plezi shortcodes in WordPress? =

To publish a form online, copy its shortcode and paste it in a classic text bloc.
