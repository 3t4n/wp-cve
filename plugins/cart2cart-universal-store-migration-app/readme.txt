### Migration App Plugin by Cart2Cart. How It Works

You don’t need any technical knowledge to migrate products to WooCommerce – just follow a few steps on the Cart2Cart Migration Wizard and your data will be transferred automatically:

1. Specify your Source store & WooCommerce store details (follow the wizard instructions for your source store, to enable data exchange between them).
2. Choose the data entities to migrate & additional migration options.
3. Perform a Free Demo migration (optional) or start a Full Migration.

**Cart2Cart: Migration App plugin is free to download and after installation, you will get 2 migration options:**

- **Demo Migration** (free) – lets you move a limited number of entities from your current store to a live WooCommerce store (or test Cart2Cart store) within 30 minutes.
- **Full Migration** (paid) – allows moving an UNLIMITED number of source eCommerce entities to WooCommerce retaining all associated information.

==Installation==

1. Download the plugin.
1. Log in to your WordPress admin panel
1. Go to Plugins > Add New > Browse. Pick the Cart2Cart plugin and click the “Install Now” button.
1. Activate it by clicking “Activate plugin”.
1. Find the Cart2Cart plugin on the left side menu. Register Cart2Cart and proceed with the migration setup.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can Cart2Cart transfer customers to WooCommerce? =
To automatically migrate customer data to WooCommerce, all you have to do is to check the matching box (Customers) on the appropriate Migration Wizard step (Select Entities for Migration).

= Is it possible to migrate metadata to WooCommerce? =
Sure. The Cart2Cart: Migration App supports metadata migration across these shopping carts. More specifically, you can transfer meta titles, keywords, and descriptions to your new WooCommerce store. However, keep in mind that the [WordPress SEO Plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wordpress-seo) (it’s free) should be installed before the migration.

= Is SEO migration available to WooCommerce? =
Absolutely. If you want to migrate your store SEO rankings to the WooCommerce platform, you should choose the Migrate categories and products SEO URLs additional option in the Migration Wizard.
