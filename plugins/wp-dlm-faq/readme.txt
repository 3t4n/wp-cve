Add a faq on any of your page/post using the simple shortcode [inject-faq].

== Description ==
An easy way to **add a faq** on one of your pages becomes reality thanks to this WordPress plugin. Just use the shortcode [inject-faq] on any of your pages. This will automatically generate a faq section of all your pages and posts.
