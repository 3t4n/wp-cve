A simple plugin to offer discounts based on product categories in WooCommerce. Offer a percentage or flat discount for products from one or more WooCommerce Product Categories.

== Description ==

Category Discounts for WooCommerce allows you to offer bulk discounts at the category level. Offer a percentage or a flat discount for products in one or multiple categories. Encourage customers to shop more by displaying discount messages on the product page.

NOTE: It is a simple discount plugin. It aims to offer a product category-wide discount.

== Why you should provide a category specific discount? ==

A best discount strategy on top-selling product categories can boost your revenue 3x times than providing a coupon based discounts. Also applying the discount automatically will ensure a faster checkout experience.
Coupons adds a friction in the checkout process as customer has to remember and then enter a coupon code.

**Added advantages of creating targeted discounts on a particular product category:**

* Boosts Sale conversions
* Sell your inventory faster
* Increases traffic to your store


== Key Features ==

* Category level discounts
* Discount can be either a percentage or a flat amount
* Automatically apply the discount in the cart/checkout
* Show a discount applied message on the cart page
* Set expiry for offers
* Multiple discount offers can be created. Example 10% for Category A, 20% for Category B

== Examples ==

* Get a 10% bulk discount for Product Category Accessories.
* Get 25% discount on Category A and Category B
* Shoes get 10% and Bags get 20% OFF
* $10 discount for any products in Category A

== Installation ==
Just use the WordPress installer or upload to the /wp-content/plugins folder. Then Activate the Category Discounts for WooCommerce plugin.

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 4.4.1 or greater
* WooCommerce 2.6.1 or greater
* PHP version 5.6.0 or greater
* MySQL version 5.0 or greater

== Frequently asked questions ==

= Can I set a validity for the discount offer ? =

Yes. You can set up an expiry date for the offer. You can also schedule an offer to get activated at a future date.

= Are the discounts visible in the product page ? =

You can show a crossed-out original price along with a discounted price.

= Can I create multiple offers? =

Yes. You can create any number of offers using the plugin.
