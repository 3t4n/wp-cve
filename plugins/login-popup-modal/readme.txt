All Login Form facilita el acceso a los usuarios en cualquier página.

== Description ==

&laquo;All Login Form&raquo; plugin allows your users login to site without leaving the same page that they are viewing. This improves user experience and engagement on your online store, membership website or learning management system.

The login modal form is mobile friendly, multilingual and translation ready.

You can:

* Add login/logout link in your current WordPress menus or place a shortcode anywhere.
* Setup login and logout redirects.
* Redirect users after login or logout to a particular page or promotional page.
* Customize title form, login inputs, button or error messages in more advanced way with CSS.
* Add your own logo of company and brand.

Ask your questions in the support forum, or [contact us](https://superadmin.es/contacto/) directly.
