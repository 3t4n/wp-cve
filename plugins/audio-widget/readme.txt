Add Audio to your website with audio widget Simply.

== Description ==

Add Audio to your WordPress website with **Audio widget** Simply. **Audio widget** uses WordPress media manager to add audio and poster to your sidebar. So it’s clean and user friendly.
 
Also You can add these options to your audio:

*	write a **Title** for your Audio;
*	You can add an **image Poster** to your Audio, so you keep the viewer’s attention;
*	You can write an **audio Description** If you want;
*	You can **Link** your **audio title and image** to the other pages and open them in the same or new windows.
*	You can **add Controls** to your audio, so your website’s viewers can play, pause, seek and download your audio.

Do you want more options?

*	You can **loop** your audio;
*	You can choose to **autoplay** it, when your website is loaded.
*	And **preload** your audio. It Means your audio will be loaded with your website at the same time.

And more …

I will support you in the support section of **Audio widget** plugin.

What are you waiting for?

Download **Audio widget** now and join with other website managers to surprise your website’s viewers. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/audio-widget` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the Audio Widget through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Go to Appearance > Widget and use Audio Widget
