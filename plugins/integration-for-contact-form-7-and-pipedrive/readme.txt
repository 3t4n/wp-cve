Send Contact Form 7, WPForms, Elementor, Ninja Forms, CRM Perks Forms and many other contact form submissions to Pipedrive.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 Pipedrive extension sends form submissions from [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/), [WPforms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpforms-lite/), [Elementor Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/elementor/), [Ninja Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/), [Formidable Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/formidable/), [Contact Form Entries](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-entries/) and many other popular contact form plugins to Pipedrive when anyone submits a form. Learn more at [crmperks.com](https://www.crmperks.com/plugins/contact-form-plugins/contact-form-pipedrive-plugin/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=pipedrive_readme)


== Contact form 7 Pipedrive integration ==

* Go to "Pipedrive Accounts" tab then add new account.
* Go to "Pipedrive Feeds" tab then create a new feed.
* Map required pipedrive fields to contact form fields.
* Send your test entry to pipedrive by clicking "send to pipedrive" button.
* Go to "Pipedrive Logs" tab and verify status of previously sent entry.


**Connect Pipedrive CRM account**

Connect any contact form 7 to Pipedrive account by simply entering Pipedrive API key. Additionally, you can connect multiple Pipedrive accounts to Contact Form 7.

**Map Pipedrive fields**

Select any pipedrive Object then Map pipedrive Object fields to any contact form 7 fields. Plugin displays  all pipedrive fields.

**Filter contact form 7  submissions**

Filter contact form 7 submissions sent to Pipedrive based on user input. For example , send those customers to Pipedrive who check "Subscribe to newsletter" checkbox.  

**Manually send to Pipedrive**

Send contact form 7 submissions to Pipedrive when someone submits a contact form. You can manually send contact form 7 submissions to Pipedrive by clicking "Send to Pipedrive" button.

**Pipedrive logs**

View a detailed log of each contact form 7 submission Whether sent or not sent to Pipedrive and easily resend contact form 7 submission to Pipedrive.

**Send Data As Pipedrive Contact Notes**

Send one to many contact form 7 form fields as Pipedrive Object notes when someone submits a contact form on your site.

**Create Or Update Contact in Pipedrive**

If a Contact already exists in Pipedrive , update it otherwise create a new Contact in Pipedrive.

= How to integrate contact form 7 with pipedrive =

You can easily integrate contact form 7 with pipedrive by following these steps.

* Go to Pipedrive Accounts then connect your account.
* Go to Pipedrive Feeds , select desired Object then map form fields to pipedrive Object fields.
* Submit a contact form , a new member will added in pipedrive.
* If you have installed Contact Form Entries plugin then open any entry and click "Send to Pipedrive" button.
* Go to Pipedrive logs and check if entry was successfully sent to Pipedrive.

= WPForms Pipedrive =

* Easily connect WPforms to our WPForms Pipedrive Integration Plugin.
* Create feeds for Pipedrive Contacts, Companies and Leads etc.
* New WPForms submission will be automatically sent to your Pipedrive account.

= Elementor form Pipedrive =

* Easily Connect Elementor forms to your Pipedrive account.
* Create a feed for Pipedrive Contacts, Companies and Leads etc, select correct Elementor form and finally map fields in feed.
* When someone submits Elementor form, submission will be automatically sent to your Pipedrive account.
* You can manually send any Elementor forms entry to Pipedrive by clicking "send to Pipedrive" button.

= Ninja Forms Pipedrive =

* Easily connect Ninja Forms to your Pipedrive account.
* After connecting Pipedrive account, create a feed for Contacts, Companies and Leads etc.
* New Ninja Forms submission will be automatically sent to your Pipedrive account.
* You can manually send any Ninja Forms entry to Pipedrive by clicking "send to Pipedrive" button.

= Formidable Forms Pipedrive =

* Connect your Pipedrive account with our Pipedrive plugin.
* Create feeds for any Pipedrive Object like Contacts, Companies and Leads etc. Select correct Formidable Form in feed. 
* New Formidable Form submission will be automatically sent to your Pipedrive account.
* You can manually send any Formidable Form entry to Pipedrive by clicking "send to Pipedrive" button.
