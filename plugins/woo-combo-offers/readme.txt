Combo Offers WooCommerce is a plugin help you bundle a few products with your custom quantity, offer them at a discount and watch the sales go up!

== Description ==

Combo Offers WooCommerce will allow you to offer different combo deals to your users and boost your sales. You can add a bunch of products as a combo at discounted combo price.

<strong>Key features:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Set quantity for each product</li>
<li>AJAX search & choose products</li>
<li>Drag & drop to re-arrange</li>
<li>Auto calculate regular & sale price</li>
<li>Ability to add the variable product or specifically a variation to the combo</li>
<li>Mark the combo is virtual when all sub-products are virtual</li>
<li>WPML integration</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==

1. Please make sure that you installed WooCommerce
2. Go to plugins in your dashboard and select "Add New"
3. Search for "Combo Offers Woocommerce", Install & Activate it
4. Now when you create/edit a product you can choose product type is "Combo Product", then add many products as you want
5. Done! click Save and Go to front end
