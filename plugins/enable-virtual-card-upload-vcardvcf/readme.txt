== Description ==

Introducing Enable virtual card upload - vcf/vcard, the powerful plugin that allows you to easily upload virtual cards (vCards, vcf) to your WordPress media library!

With our plugin, you can easily add vCards and VCF to your website, giving your visitors an easy way to access important contact information. Whether you're running a personal blog or a professional website, vCards are an essential tool for staying in touch with your readers and customers.

Here are some of the key features of our plugin:

Easy vCard and vFcard Upload: With just a few clicks, you can upload vCards and vFcards to your WordPress media library, making them easily accessible to your visitors.

Compatible with All Major vCard Formats: our plugin supports all major vCard formats, including vcf,vcard so you can easily upload and use any vCard file you have.

Enable virtual card upload - vcf/vcard is the perfect plugin for anyone looking to add vCards and VCF to their WordPress website. With its powerful features and easy-to-use interface, you can quickly and easily add important contact information to your website, making it easy for your visitors to get in touch with you.

So why wait? Download Enable virtual card upload - vcf,vcard today and start adding vCards and VCF to your WordPress website with ease!

== Installation ==

Installing "Enable Virtual Card Upload - Vcard,Vcf" can be done either by searching for "Enable Virtual Card Upload - Vcard,Vcf" via the "Plugins > Add New" screen in your WordPress dashboard, or by using the following steps:

1. Download the plugin via WordPress.org
1. Upload the ZIP file through the 'Plugins > Add New > Upload' screen in your WordPress dashboard
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
