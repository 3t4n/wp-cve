== Description ==

**bbPress Google reCAPTCHA v2** is a free plugin/add-on for bbPress, that allow you to easily add **Google reCAPTCHA** v2 on your bbPress forum.

**bbPress Google reCAPTCHA v2** prevent spam from creating new topics or replies. This plugin is built to integrate seamlessly with your bbPress enabled WordPress website.

**bbPress Google reCAPTCHA v2** is very easy to use and work for **Anonymous posting** as well as **Logged-in users**.
