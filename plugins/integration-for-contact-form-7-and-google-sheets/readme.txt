Send Contact Form 7, WPForms, Elementor, Ninja Forms, Contact Form Entries Plugin and many other contact form submissions to Google Sheets.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 Google Sheets Plugin sends form submissions from [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/), [WPforms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpforms-lite/), [Elementor Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/elementor/), [Ninja Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/), [Formidable Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/formidable/), [Contact Form Entries](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-entries/) and many other popular contact form plugins to Google Sheets when someone submits a form. Learn more about Contact Form Google Sheets Plugin at [crmperks.com](https://www.crmperks.com/plugins/contact-form-plugins/contact-form-google-sheets-integration/?utm_source=wordpress&amp;utm_medium=directory&amp;utm_campaign=Google_Sheets_readme). 

== How to Setup ==

* Go to "Google Sheets Accounts" tabÂ then add new account.
* Go to "Google Sheets Feeds" tab then create new feed.
* Map required Google Sheets fields to contact form 7 fields.
* Send your test entry to Google Sheets.
* Go to "Google Sheets Logs" tab and verify, if entry was sent to Google Sheets.

**Connecting Google Sheets account**

Connect any Contact Form 7 form to Google Sheets by safe and secure Oauth 2.0. Additionally, you can connect multiple Google Sheets to Contact Form 7.

**Map Google Sheets fields**

Select any Google Sheet then Map Contact Form 7 form fields to Google Sheet fields.

**Filter Contact Form 7 Google Sheets entries**

Either send all entries to Google Sheets or send specific entries based on user input. For example , only send those form entries to Google Sheets which contanin work email address.

**Manually sending entries to Google Sheets**

You can automatically Send Contact Form 7 submissions to Google Sheets when a user submits a form. Also, you can manually send Contact Form 7 submissions to Google Sheets.

**Contact Form 7 Google Sheets logs**

View a detailed log of each Contact Form 7 submission Whether sent or not sent to Google Sheets , You can easily resend any entry to Google Sheets.

= WPForms Google sheets =

* Easily connect WPforms to any Google sheet.
* Create feeds for Google sheet Contacts, Companies and Tickets etc.
* New WPforms submission will be automatically sent to your Google sheet account.

= Elementor forms to Google sheet =

* Easily Connect Elementor forms to your required Google sheet.
* Create a feed for any sheet, select correct Elementor form and finally map fields in feed.
* When someone submits Elementor form, submission will be automatically sent to your Google sheet.
* You can manually send any Elementor forms entry to Google sheet by clicking "send to Google sheet" button.

= Contact Form 7 Google Sheet integration =
* You can directly save Contact Form 7 entries to Google sheets.
* Simply Select Google sheet then map form fields to Google sheet fields.
* Contact Form 7 entry will be added to Google sheet when anyone submits a form on your site.

= Ninja Forms Google sheets =

* Easily connect Ninja Forms to your Google sheet account.
* After connecting Google sheet account, create a feed for any sheet, finally map fields in feed.
* New Ninja Forms submission will be automatically sent to your Google sheet.
* You can manually send any Ninja Forms entry to Google sheet by clicking "send to Google sheet" button.

= Formidable Forms Google sheet =

* Connect your Google sheet account with our plugin.
* Create feeds for any Google sheet. Select correct Formidable Form in feed. 
* New Formidable Form submission will be automatically sent to your Google sheet.
* You can manually send any Formidable Form entry to Google sheet by clicking "send to Google sheet" button.

= Connect contact form 7 to Google sheets =
* First connect your google sheets account with Contact Form 7 Google sheets Plugin.
* Create a feed and map form fields to Google sheet fields in feed.
* All form submissions will be automatically sent as Google sheets rows.

= Contact Form 7 to Google Sheets =
* You can easily save Contact Form 7 entries to Google Google Sheets.
* Simply install this free Contact Form 7 Google sheets plugin, map form fields to Google sheet fields.
* All form submissions will be automatically saved to your selected Google Sheet.
