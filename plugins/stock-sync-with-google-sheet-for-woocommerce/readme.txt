== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why I can't sync my store all my products with Google Sheet?
If your store product is more than 100 then you'll need to upgrade to the [ULTIMATE version](https://go.wppool.dev/Kr7y) to sync all your store product.

= What to do with the variable products of my store?
Stock Sync with Google Sheet for WooCommerce plugin will automatically create rows for your variable products in the sheet and you can manage them smoothly.

= How can I link my site with Google Sheet?
It's quite simple and easy. You'll get directions in every step after activating the plugin. (Check our tutorial)[https://youtu.be/frwhDkk6sa4].

= Can I add any product from the Google Sheet?
Yes! With Stock Sync with Google Sheet for WooCommerce Ultimate, you can instantly add new products to your store from Google Sheet.

= Can I remove any product from the Google Sheet?
No. You'll need to update the data from your site dashboard to remove a product from your store. We are adding this restriction to prevent unwanted changes from the spreadsheet.

= Can I manage the product SKU with the plugin??
Yes. The plugin will automatically sync SKU, just like other product data. Even you can edit it from Google Sheets and it will be live synced with WooCommerce.

= How can I add the newly added product to the Google Sheet?
Newly added products are supposed to Sync with Google Sheet automatically. Unless, click the 'Sync with Google Sheet' button at 'Products' page from your website dashboard. It's as simple as that. Also, you can follow Google Sheets > Find the tab 'Stock Sync' > Click on 'Fetch from WooCommerce'.

= Do I need to know any programming language for using this plugin?
Nope! No coding knowledge is required for using this plugin.
