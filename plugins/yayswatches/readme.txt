== Description ==

YaySwatches allows you to show variation selectors as color swatches, image swatches, radio buttons, buttons, and more. Let your customers easily switch between product variations on product details page!

Showing **WooCommerce color swatches** for customers to choose from makes a way better shopping experience.

By transforming your variation dropdown lists into more appealing designs, you will pique the interest of the visitors and turn them into customers.

###⚡️ FEATURES

YaySwatches is a feature-packed **WooCommerce variation swatches** plugin with a modern and simplified user interface. It combines pre-existing attributes and custom product attributes in a single dashboard. Just visit YaySwatches options and settings, you will see all available product attributes and choose the styles you like.

**Color Swatches**
Displaying colour swatches based on manually picked colours.

**Automated Variation Image Swatches**
Displaying swatches based on different variant images. Lateral product variation images appear when clicking on the corresponding swatch.

**Customizable Swatch Bubbles**
Plenty of design options including swatch shape, size, border, color, and variation attribute tooltip.

**Customizable Button**
You can tweak the variation button design to fit your website's color profiles. Even better, you can display buttons for attributes like size, material, model, etc. right on shop/category page.

**Powerful Attribute Options**
YaySwatches works with pre-existing attributes (free version) and also custom product attributes (premium version only).

**Enhanced Shop Page (PRO)**
Shop and category pages are fully supported in [YaySwatches Pro](https://yaycommerce.com/yayswatches-variation-swatches-for-woocommerce/) version. You can enable and set a limit to the number of swatch bubbles shown for each product on the shop page. This helps keep your product grid neat and tidy. Once the customer clicks on the rest of the swatch options, they can view the remaining swatches or jump to product page.

**Out-of-stock Variation Visibility (PRO)**
Show or hide those variations that have been sold out on product page, shop page, and categories. You can choose a predesigned style for showing/hiding variations to fit your WooCommerce store.

**Making Your Products Easy to Find**
The ultimate goal of this plugin is to make browsing your products or services extremely easy.

### QUICK & EASY SETUP
With YaySwatches - Variation Swatches for WooCommerce plugin, it takes a few minutes to set up all WooCommerce variation swatches. It provides a more vivid appearance for your product attributes through color swatch, image swatch, automated variant image swatch, button, and radio button.
As a result, all of the items are displayed so that your customer can quickly get all the available product options you have to offer.

With YaySwatches, the customers can browse and buy your products with engagement!

To further enhance your ecommerce site, you can even [send WooCommerce custom email per product](https://yaycommerce.com/how-to-send-woocommerce-custom-emails-per-product/), product variations or product SKUs.

== Installation ==
Setup is easy and takes less than one minute.

1. Upload the plugin zip file to WordPress Plugins menu.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
3. Go to WooCommerce > YaySwatches to start configuring WooCommerce variation swatches.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is YaySwatches plugin compatible with any WooCommerce themes? =
Yes, it goes along well with all popular WooCommerce themes. In some cases, it may require additional tweaks to make it work seamlessly with your current theme.

= How to use color swatches for those custom product attributes that are not added to "Attribute" menu?=
This feature is available in [YaySwatches Pro](https://yaycommerce.com/yayswatches-variation-swatches-for-woocommerce/). You can go to WooCommerce admin > Products > Edit product and look for **YaySwatches Settings**, there you can find the current custom product attribute terms and design their variant selector types.

= I got issues. How can I get support? =
Please [create a topic](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/yayswatches) or [contact us](https://yaycommerce.com/support/) to get help.
Please attach screenshots of currently active WooCommerce plugins on your website to quickly get the answers. Maybe it is a third-party conflict.

= Is YaySwatches GDPR-compliant? =
A big yes, this WooCommerce variation swatches plugin is compliant with GDPR. It collects no sensitive information.
