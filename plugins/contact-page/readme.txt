Easily create a contact page with relevant address information, Google Maps, your latest tweets and links to relevant social media profiles.

== Description ==

Create a contact page that is easy to maintain, with some special features like fully customizable Google Maps integration and Twitter feed.

* Google Maps
* Twitter
* Facebook
* LinkedIn


== Installation ==

1. Place the 'contact-page' folder in your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
1. Activate 'Contact Page'.
1. Go to 'Settings > Contact Page' and fill in your contact details.
1. Add the shortcode `[contact-page]` to any page (or post).
1. Have a look at your site.
