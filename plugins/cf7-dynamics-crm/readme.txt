Send Contact Form 7, WPForms, Elementor, Ninja Forms, CRM Perks Forms and many other contact form submissions to dynamics crm Online.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 Dynamics CRM Plugin sends form submissions from [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/), [WPforms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpforms-lite/), [Elementor Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/elementor/), [Ninja Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/), [Formidable Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/formidable/), [Contact Form Entries](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-entries/) and many other popular contact form plugins to Dynamics CRM. Learn more at [crmperks.com](https://www.crmperks.com/plugins/contact-form-plugins/contact-form-dynamics-plugin/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=dynamics_readme)


== How to Setup ==

* Go to "Dynamics CRM Accounts" tab and add new account.
* Go to "Dynamics CRM Feeds" tab , create new feed.
* Map required Dynamics CRM fields to contact form 7 fields.
* Send your test entry to Dynamics CRM.
* Go to "Dynamics CRM Logs" tab and verify, if entry was sent to Dynamics CRM.

== Dynamics CRM On-premises ==

Contact Form 7 Dynamics CRM add-on only supports Microsoft Dynamics CRM Online.


**Connect Microsoft Dynamics CRM account**

Connect any contact form 7 to Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 account by safe and secure Oauth 2.0. Additionally, you can connect multiple Dynamics 365 crm accounts

**Map Microsoft Dynamics CRM fields**

In a feed, select Dynamics CRM Object then Map any contact form 7 fields to Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 object(Contacts, Account, Lead, Order, Case) fields.

**Filter contact form 7  submissions**

Send all or filter contact form 7 submissions sent to Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 based on user input. For example , only send that entry to Dynamics CRM which contains work email address.

**Manually send entries to Dynamics CRM**

Send contact form 7 submissions to Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 when someone submit a form. You can manually send contact form submissions to Dynamics CRM.

**Dynamics CRM logs**

View a detailed log of each contact form 7 submission whether sent or not sent to Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 and easily resend contact form 7 submission to Dynamics CRM.

**Send Data As Dynamics CRM object Notes**

Send one to many contact form 7 fields as Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 object(Contacts, Account, Lead, Order, Case) notes.


**Create Or Update Contact in Dynamics CRM**

If an entry(Contacts, Account, Lead, Order, Case) already exists in Dynamics CRM/Dynamics 365 , update it otherwise create a new entry in Dynamics CRM.

= Contact Form 7 Dynamics CRM Integration =

Contact Form 7 Dynamics CRM Integration or Contact Form 7 Dynamics 365 Integration supports following tasks.

* Integrate Contact Form 7 to Dynamics CRM Contacts.
* Integrate Contact Form 7 to Dynamics CRM Accounts.
* Integrate Contact Form 7 to Dynamics CRM Leads.
* Integrate Contact Form 7 to Dynamics CRM Orders.
* Integrate Contact Form 7 to Dynamics CRM Cases.

= WPForms Dynamics CRM =

* Easily connect WPforms to any Dynamics CRM account.
* Create feeds for Dynamics CRM Contacts, Companies and Leads etc.
* New WPforms submission will be automatically sent to your Dynamics CRM account.

= Elementor forms to Dynamics CRM =

* Easily Connect Elementor forms to your required Dynamics CRM account.
* Create a feed for any CRM entity, select correct Elementor form and finally map fields in feed.
* When someone submits Elementor form, submission will be automatically sent to your Dynamics CRM.
* You can manually send any Elementor forms entry to Dynamics CRM by clicking "send to Dynamics CRM" button.

= Wordpress Dynamics CRM Integration =

Easily integrate wordpress to Dynamics CRM or Dynamics 365 with Contact form 7 Dyanmics crm/Dynamics 365 Plugin.

* Create a Contact in Dynamics crm on new contact form 7 submission.
* Create a Lead in Dynamics crm on new contact form 7 submission.
* Create or update a case in Dynamics crm on new contact form 7 submission.
* Create or update a account in Dynamics crm on new contact form 7 submission.
* Create or update an Order in Dynamics crm on new contact form 7 submission.

= Ninja Forms Dynamics CRM =

* Easily connect Ninja Forms to your Dynamics CRM account.
* After connecting Dynamics CRM account, create a feed for Contacts, Companies and Leads etc.
* New Ninja Forms submission will be automatically sent to your Dynamics CRM account.
* You can manually send any Ninja Forms entry to Dynamics CRM by clicking "send to Dynamics CRM" button.

= Formidable Forms Dynamics CRM =

* Connect your Dynamics CRM account with our Dynamics CRM plugin.
* Create feeds for any Dynamics CRM list like Contacts, Companies and Leads etc. Select correct Formidable Form in feed. 
* New Formidable Form submission will be automatically sent to your Dynamics CRM account.
* You can manually send any Formidable Form entry to Dynamics CRM by clicking "send to Dynamics CRM" button.
